Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions, clear answers.

How much can I borrow?+
Your borrowing limit depends on your income, existing financial commitments, and the property value. As a general guide, most applicants can borrow up to 4.5 times their gross annual income. Use our calculator to get a personalised estimate.
What is the minimum deposit required?+
For first-time buyers, the minimum deposit is 5% of the property value. For home movers and remortgage applicants, a minimum of 10% is required. A larger deposit typically means access to better interest rates.
How long does the application process take?+
Once we receive your completed application and all required documents, we aim to provide a decision within 5 working days. The full process from application to completion typically takes 4–8 weeks, depending on the complexity of your situation.
Can I make overpayments?+
Yes. Most of our mortgage products allow overpayments of up to 10% of the outstanding balance per year without penalty. This can significantly reduce the total interest you pay over the life of the mortgage.
What happens if I miss a payment?+
If you think you may miss a payment, contact us as early as possible. We have a dedicated support team who can discuss your options, including payment deferrals or restructuring your repayment plan. Missing payments without notice can affect your credit rating and may result in additional charges.
